Landscape of human fear in Neotropical rainforest mammals

This repository provides some data and codes to replicate the analysis used in the manuscript "Landscape of human fear in Neotropical rainforest mammals".
The data provided here is purposefuly limited to avoid authorship conflicts. So, small changes in relation to the manuscript results may occur.
